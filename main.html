About ohSTEM

Organization of Honoraries in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (ohSTEM) is a professional society for graduate and undergraduate New York University students in STEM related fields. Our goal is to give students all the necessary tools and connections needed to succeed in a professional environnment. With more than 3 different student chapters at colleges/universities all over the Tri-State Metropolitan Area, ohSTEM stands as the most efficient organization focused on students in STEM related fields.

ohSTEM Vision

We envision a world where students of the new generation are empowered to achieve success in a safe and supportive environnement that celebrates diversity and unique contributions.

ohSTEM Mission

ohSTEM empowers students in STEM related fields to succeed personally, academically, and professionally by cultivating environnements and communities that nurture innovation and leadership.

ohSTEM Values

  • Integrity

    We establish trust through honesty and transparency by maintaining the highest standard of ethics.

  • Respect

    We create and maintain openness and inclusion, appreciating the individual contributions and fostering diversity and equity.

  • Passion

    We are dedicated to the relentless pursuit of our vision.

  • Perseverance

    We push forward with courage and determination, regardless of the circumstances, to achieve our mission
